Brigadeiro Recipe

Country: Brazil

Preparing time: 1 hour

Difficulty: ★☆☆

Servings: 16

Indulge in the sweet delight of Brazilian brigadeiros, a beloved chocolate treat that's both creamy and fudgy. With an easy preparation process, these treats can be made by anyone, whether you're a seasoned baker or just starting out. Capture the spirit of Brazil and treat yourself to the irresistible allure of homemade brigadeiros.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles, for coating

Instructions

  1. In a non-stick saucepan, combine the sweetened condensed milk, unsweetened cocoa powder, and unsalted butter.
  2. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring continuously to prevent sticking or burning.
  3. Continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens and pulls away from the sides of the pan. It should have a fudgy consistency.
  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the mixture cool for a few minutes. It will become easier to handle.
  5. Grease your hands with butter or oil to prevent sticking. Take small portions of the mixture and roll them into bite-sized balls.
  6. Roll the brigadeiros in chocolate sprinkles, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  7. Place the brigadeiros in small paper or foil cups for serving, if desired.
  8. Allow the brigadeiros to cool completely before enjoying. They can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature.
