About Us

Experience a fusion of flavors and cultures with Natalie Limberger and Parawee Tamee, two dessert enthusiasts from opposite corners of the world. We're passionate about sharing the most delicious dessert creations from across the globe. Our love for desserts and exploration led us to create Global Desserts, a digital haven for fellow dessert enthusiasts. We believe that desserts are a universal language that bridges cultures, creating connections that transcend geographical boundaries. Discover a curated collection of recipes that traverse continents, bringing the world's sweetest treasures to your kitchen.
