Classic Tiramisu

Country: Italy

Preparing time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: ★★☆

Servings: 8

Indulge in the rich and irresistible delight of these fudgy chocolate brownies. These brownies strike the perfect balance between a crisp outer layer and a dense, gooey interior. They are a true crowd-pleaser, whether you're baking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself.

Ingredients

  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 8 oz (227g) mascarpone cheese
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24-30 ladyfinger biscuits
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. In a heatproof bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and 1/2 cup of granulated sugar. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) and whisk continuously until the mixture is pale, thick, and forms ribbons when the whisk is lifted. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  2. In another bowl, beat the mascarpone cheese until smooth and creamy.
  3. Gently fold the mascarpone into the egg yolk mixture until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and remaining 1/4 cup of granulated sugar until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gradually fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until no streaks remain. Add vanilla extract and gently combine.
  6. Mix the brewed coffee and coffee liqueur (if using) in a shallow dish.
  7. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture, ensuring they are moistened but not soggy. Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish.
  8. Spread half of the mascarpone cream mixture over the ladyfingers.
  9. Create another layer by dipping and arranging a second layer of soaked ladyfingers, followed by the remaining mascarpone cream mixture.
  10. Cover the tiramisu with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, allowing the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.
  11. Just before serving, dust the top of the tiramisu with unsweetened cocoa powder using a fine sieve.
  12. Slice and serve the classic tiramisu, savoring the luxurious blend of coffee, creamy mascarpone, and cocoa.
